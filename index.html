Secure and Reliable System Research Lab

Department of Computer Science, University of Colorado Colorado Springs
This lab aims to enhance the security and reliability of the systems via new novel solutions at the compiler, OS, and communication layers. The systems we target include microcontroller- and microprocessor-based systems.
